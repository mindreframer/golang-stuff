About Us

Welcome to Conroy’s Alhambra. We are located at the corner of Valley Blvd and Atlantic Ave. We have a wide selection of flowers, houseplants, plush and other gift items. We specialize in weddings and funerals. We deliver to Alhambra, Monterey Park, Altadena, Arcadia Baldwin Park, Boyle Heights, Bradbury, City Terrace, Covina, Duarte, East LA, El Monte, El Creno, Forest Lawn Covina, Highland Park, Irwindale, La Puente, Lincoln Heights, Los Angeles, Monrovia, Montebello, Pasadena, Rosemead, Rose Hills, San Gabriel, San Marino, Serra Madera, South El Monte, South Pasadena, South San Gabriel, and Temple City. Have the ability to deliver around the block and around the world. Stop in and see our designer choice mixed arrangements. 10 percent off next purchase.
